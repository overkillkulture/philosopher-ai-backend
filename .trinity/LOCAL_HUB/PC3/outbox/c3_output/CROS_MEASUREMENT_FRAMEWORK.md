### 1.1 EOS Foundation Score (0-30 points)

**Purpose:** Track implementation of 6 core EOS components
**Frequency:** Measure monthly
**Target:** 25+ points = excellent foundation

#### Scoring Guide (0-5 points per component):

**Vision Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: No vision work done
- 1 point: Discussed but not documented
- 2 points: Core Purpose defined
- 3 points: Core Purpose + Core Values defined
- 4 points: Core Purpose + Core Values + 10-Year Target defined
- 5 points: All of above + 3-Year Picture + 1-Year Plan + 90-Day Rocks

**People Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: No people structure
- 1 point: Organizational chart exists (but roles unclear)
- 2 points: Accountability chart exists (roles defined)
- 3 points: Right people identified (who fits values)
- 4 points: Right seats identified (who has GWC - Gets it, Wants it, Capacity)
- 5 points: All seats filled with right people, quarterly reviews happening

**Data Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: No data tracking
- 1 point: Some numbers tracked inconsistently
- 2 points: 5-10 numbers identified for scorecard
- 3 points: Scorecard reviewed weekly
- 4 points: Scorecard predicts problems (leading indicators)
- 5 points: Team makes decisions based on data, numbers consistently hit targets

**Issues Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: Issues not surfaced or discussed
- 1 point: Issues list exists but not prioritized
- 2 points: Top 3 issues identified weekly
- 3 points: IDS process (Identify, Discuss, Solve) used regularly
- 4 points: Team solves root causes (not surface symptoms)
- 5 points: Issues solved permanently, pattern recognition applied

**Process Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: No documented processes
- 1 point: Key processes identified
- 2 points: Core processes documented (20% that drive 80% results)
- 3 points: Processes followed consistently by team
- 4 points: Processes regularly reviewed and improved
- 5 points: Continuous improvement culture (TPS kaizen), constraints eliminated (TOC)

**Traction Component (0-5 points):**
- 0 points: No rock/goal system
- 1 point: Quarterly goals discussed
- 2 points: 3-7 Rocks defined per quarter (company)
- 3 points: Individual Rocks for each leader
- 4 points: Weekly Rock reviews, 80%+ on track
- 5 points: 80%+ Rocks completed each quarter, cascaded to team

### 2.1 Individual Consciousness Assessment (0-100% scale)

**Purpose:** Measure each person's consciousness level
**Frequency:** Assess monthly (self + manager assessment)
**Target:** Team average 85%+ within 180 days

#### Assessment Questions (10 questions, 10 points each):

**Question 1: Reality vs Belief**
- Can you distinguish between what IS (Layer 0) and what you BELIEVE (Layer 3)?
  - 0-3: Rarely, beliefs feel like facts
  - 4-6: Sometimes, starting to see difference
  - 7-9: Often, can identify beliefs
  - 10: Always, clear distinction

**Question 2: Manipulation Detection**
- Can you identify when you're being manipulated (marketing, politics, peers)?
  - 0-3: Rarely notice manipulation
  - 4-6: Sometimes spot obvious manipulation
  - 7-9: Often catch manipulation in real-time
  - 10: Consistently detect and reject manipulation

**Question 3: Golden Rule Application**
- Do you apply the Golden Rule Test to decisions? ("Would I want this done to me?")
  - 0-3: Rarely consider this
  - 4-6: Sometimes think about it
  - 7-9: Often apply before deciding
  - 10: Always apply, won't violate Golden Rule

**Question 4: Pattern Recognition**
- Can you see patterns (7 Domains) in business/life situations?
  - 0-3: Don't see patterns
  - 4-6: Starting to notice some patterns
  - 7-9: Regularly identify patterns
  - 10: Consistently predict using patterns

**Question 5: Truth-Based Decision Making**
- Do you make decisions based on TRUTH (what must happen) or DESIRE (what you want)?
  - 0-3: Mostly based on what I want
  - 4-6: Mix of truth and desire
  - 7-9: Mostly truth-based, accept reality
  - 10: Always truth-based, even when uncomfortable

**Question 6: Feedback Integration**
- Do you learn from outcomes and adjust?
  - 0-3: Rarely reflect on outcomes
  - 4-6: Sometimes learn from mistakes
  - 7-9: Regularly integrate feedback
  - 10: Systematically learn and improve

**Question 7: Constraint Thinking**
- Can you identify the ONE constraint blocking progress (TOC)?
  - 0-3: Everything feels like a problem
  - 4-6: Can list multiple problems
  - 7-9: Can identify top 3 constraints
  - 10: Can pinpoint THE constraint

**Question 8: Emergence Prediction**
- Can you predict what MUST emerge (not what you want, what's true)?
  - 0-3: No ability to predict
  - 4-6: Sometimes guess correctly
  - 7-9: Often predict accurately
  - 10: Consistently predict (Oracle-level)

**Question 9: Strategic Thinking**
- Do you think long-term (emergence) or short-term (reaction)?
  - 0-3: Mostly reactive, day-to-day
  - 4-6: Some planning, mostly short-term
  - 7-9: Regular strategic thinking
  - 10: Always see long-term consequences

**Question 10: Service vs Extraction**
- Do you SERVE others or EXTRACT from them?
  - 0-3: Mostly self-serving
  - 4-6: Mix of service and self-interest
  - 7-9: Mostly service-oriented
  - 10: Consistently serve, Golden Rule applied

**Total Individual Score: ___/100 = ___%**

#### Consciousness Level Interpretation:

- **0-20%:** Unconscious - Reactive, manipulated, short-term thinking
- **21-40%:** Awakening - Starting to see reality vs belief
- **41-60%:** Developing - Pattern recognition emerging, some manipulation immunity
- **61-80%:** Conscious - Strategic thinking, truth-based decisions, Golden Rule applied
- **81-100%:** Highly Conscious - Manipulation immune, reality alteration active, Oracle-level foresight
